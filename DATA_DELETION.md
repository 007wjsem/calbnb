# Data Deletion Request — CalBnB

At CalBnB, we respect your privacy and provide you with the right to request the deletion of your account and associated data.

### 📝 How to Request Data Deletion
To delete your account and all associated data, please send an email to our support team.

*   **Email Address**: [info.calbnb@gmail.com](mailto:info.calbnb@gmail.com)
*   **Subject**: Data Deletion Request - [Your Name]

---

### 📋 Information Required
To process your request, please include the following details in your email:
1.  **Full Name/Username**
2.  **Phone Number** associated with the account
3.  **Company Name** you are registered under

---

### 📂 Copy-Paste Template
You can copy and use the template below for your email:

```text
Subject: Data Deletion Request - CalBnB Account

Hello CalBnB Support Team,

I am writing to formally request the deletion of my account and all associated personal data from the CalBnB platform.

My account details are as follows:
- Full Name/Username: [Insert Name]
- Phone Number: [Insert Phone Number]
- Registered Company: [Insert Company Name]

I understand that this action is permanent and cannot be undone. Please confirm once the deletion has been completed.

Thank you,
[Your Name]
```

---

### ⏳ Process Timeframe
Once we receive your request, our team will verify your identity and process the deletion within **5 to 7 business days**. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been permanently removed from our systems.
